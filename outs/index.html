Screenshots

Outs is a compact poker companion for learning decisions under pressure. Sit down with AI opponents, read your hand strength, watch equity move street by street, and climb the weekly leaderboard without real-money gambling.

Gems, chips, cosmetics, freerolls, and tournament prizes are virtual only. They exist to make practice sessions feel alive, not to create financial stakes.

Features

AI Poker Table

Fast hands against virtual opponents with readable actions, stacks, blinds, and tournament flow.

Training Signals

Starting-hand strength, equity, fold nudges for weak hands, and compact hand ranking references.

Virtual Economy

Earn and spend gems on card backs and chip colors. All rewards are virtual and non-redeemable.

Rewarded Comebacks

Optional rewarded ads help broke players return when they are below the minimum buy-in and freeroll is open.

Weekly Leaderboard

Track XP and ranking against synced players with a table that never feels empty.

Firebase Sync

Anonymous sign-in syncs balance, cosmetics, consent state, diagnostics, and progression.
