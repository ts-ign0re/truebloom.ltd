TrueBloom Ltd ("Company", "we", "us") operates the Work Remotely application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Device identifier — an anonymous, randomly generated identifier stored in your device's Keychain, used to associate your data with your device. This identifier persists across app reinstalls but is not linked to your Apple ID or any personal identity.
  • Device information — device model, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and troubleshooting.
  • Usage data — anonymous analytics about how you use the App, such as features accessed and session duration.
  • Subscription data — subscription status is managed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect or store your payment information.
  • Resume data — if you use the AI Resume Tailoring feature, the resume text you submit is stored on our servers, associated with your anonymous device identifier. This data may contain personal information such as your name, employment history, education, skills, and contact details.
  • Tailored resume outputs — AI-generated resume versions are stored on our servers, associated with your anonymous device identifier.
  • Favorites — saved job listings are stored locally on your device using SwiftData and are not transmitted to our servers.

2. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the EU/EEA and UK, we process your data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent — you provide explicit consent before submitting any resume data for AI processing. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your resume through the App.

  • Legitimate interest — we process technical and usage data to maintain and improve the App.

We use the collected information to:

  • Diagnose technical issues
