O2 is your personal breath-hold coach. Whether you're a freediver, surfer, or just curious about breathwork, our scientifically-designed training tables help you safely build CO2 and O2 tolerance over time.

Track your personal bests, log mindfulness minutes to HealthKit, and watch beautiful particle animations guide your breathing. Comprehensive safety features and health warnings ensure you train responsibly.

Features

CO₂ & O₂ Tables

Progressive training tables that adapt to your personal best, building tolerance safely.

Box Breathing

Navy SEAL 4-4-4-4 technique for stress relief and focus enhancement.

HealthKit Integration

Automatically log mindfulness minutes from your breathing exercises to Apple Health.

Haptic & Voice Cues

Train with your eyes closed using haptic feedback and voice guidance.

Beautiful Animations

Particle-based breathing ring with smooth color transitions and calming feedback.

Offline Ready

Train anywhere, anytime — no internet connection required.

Safety Notice

Breath-hold training carries inherent risks including hypoxia and loss of consciousness. Never practice in or near water without supervision. For users 18+ or with parental consent (13-18).
