Mise turns any measuring cup into a kitchen scale. Pick an ingredient, enter the amount in cups, tablespoons, or millilitres, and read it back in exact grams — because a cup of flour and a cup of honey are nowhere near the same weight.

Built for bakers and anyone cooking from recipes written in mismatched units. A 52-ingredient density and calorie database, two reading styles, and whole-recipe scaling that keeps every line exact in grams.

Features

Volume → Weight

Convert cups, spoons and millilitres to exact grams for 50+ ingredients, each with its real density.

Two Styles

Editorial — a calm, precise readout; or Tactile — a measuring glass that fills as you pour.

Recipe Scaling

Scale a whole recipe at once. Every line stays exact in grams, with clean fractions (½, ¾, ⅓).

Ingredient Library

Search, favourite, edit, and add your own ingredients with custom density and calories.

Metric or Imperial

Grams/ml or oz/fl oz, with your own cup size (200–300 ml). Calories per ingredient and per recipe.

9 Languages & VoiceOver

Fully localized into 9 languages with complete VoiceOver support. Private and offline — no account, no tracking.
