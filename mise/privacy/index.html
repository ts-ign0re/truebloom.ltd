Privacy Policy

Mise · last updated 23 June 2026

Mise collects no data. The app works entirely on your device. We do not have servers that receive your information, and we do not operate any account system.

What we collect

Nothing. Mise does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. There is no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs in the app.

What stays on your device

Your measurements, saved recipes, favourites, custom ingredients, and settings are stored locally on your device only. They are never uploaded. Deleting the app removes this data.

Photos

If you add a photo to a recipe share card, it is read only to render that image on your device and is never uploaded. Mise requests access to a photo only when you choose one.

Sharing

When you use the system share sheet to share a recipe card, the image is handed to the destination you pick (Messages, Mail, etc.). That sharing is governed by those apps, not by us.

Children

Mise is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content, no ads, and no data collection.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
