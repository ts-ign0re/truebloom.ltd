Loopix

iOS

Professional photo filter app with authentic film emulations based on real film science.

Loopix brings the magic of analog photography to your iPhone. With 50+ meticulously crafted film emulations based on measured densitometry data from real Kodak, Fuji, and Ilford stocks, every filter captures the authentic character of classic film.

Our GPU-accelerated processing engine delivers sub-200ms filter application using Core Image and custom Metal shaders. Share your unique looks with the community through our QR-based recipe system — or discover new styles from photographers worldwide.

Features

50+ Film Emulations

Authentic filters based on real Kodak Portra, Fuji Superia, Ilford HP5, and more classic stocks.

GPU Accelerated

Metal-powered processing delivers results in under 200ms. Edit in real-time without waiting.

Recipe Sharing

Create, share, and discover filter recipes via QR codes. Build your unique style library.

Pro Color Tools

Tone curves, color matrices, HSL adjustments, film grain, halation, and vignette effects.

RAW Support

Import and process RAW files with full quality. ColorChecker calibration for perfect accuracy.

Batch Export

Process entire albums with a single tap. Export to HEIC, JPEG, or PNG.

Built With

Swift 6 SwiftUI Core Image Metal PhotoKit
